## Synthesis Rules

- Use profile writers for structure and polish.
- Use recruiter/search experts for keyword and discoverability logic.
- Use creator/content experts for publishing cadence, hooks, and authority-building.
- Use LinkedIn official sources for platform claims.
- Do not copy another person's profile, post, or proprietary framework. Extract patterns and write original copy grounded in the user's evidence.
